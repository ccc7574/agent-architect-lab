# Ops And Incidents

Agent platforms need operator workflows before they need fancy dashboards.

A practical incident loop includes:

- Detect issues through dashboards, traces, and alerts.
- Triage by failure taxonomy, blast radius, and user impact.
- Rollback risky changes quickly with a clear owner.
- Preserve traces, checkpoints, and reports for review.
- Feed incident findings back into evals, safeguards, and routing policies.

Good operations turns incidents into architecture inputs instead of one-off firefights.
