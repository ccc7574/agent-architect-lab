# Evals And Safeguards

Teams building serious agent products need evals and safeguards to evolve together.

Useful operating principles:

- Slice evals by capability, risk level, and product surface.
- Treat release gates as explicit policy, not tribal knowledge.
- Review regressions with a stable failure taxonomy.
- Run local smoke tests before model-backed or shadow runs.
- Promote only when the system stays safe while quality improves.

If safeguards are not measurable and evals are not actionable, neither system is mature enough for production.
